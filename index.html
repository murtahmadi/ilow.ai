The Problem

Claims processing is broken — and getting worse.

The average insurance claim takes 25 to 38 days to close. Adjusters manually read policy documents, review damage photos by eye, and run fraud checks through disconnected systems.

Meanwhile, the adjuster workforce is retiring faster than it is being replaced. Agencies are stretched thin and mid-market carriers have no AI solution built for them — existing tools like Tractable and Lemonade serve large UK enterprises only.

  • Policy documents reviewed line-by-line by hand
  • Damage photos assessed with no structured output
  • Fraud checks done manually with spreadsheets
  • Experienced adjusters retiring with no replacement pipeline
  • No affordable AI tool exists for US independent adjusters

How It Works

Three steps.
One decision.

iLow walks adjusters through the full claim review pipeline — from intake to recommended action — in minutes.

1
Upload the Claim

Drop in a policy PDF, paste the claim summary, upload damage photos, or fill in the claim details. iLow accepts everything adjusters already have.

2
AI Analyzes Everything

iLow reads the policy language, assesses image damage type and severity, and cross-references claim details against fraud indicators — simultaneously.

3
Get a Structured Decision

Receive a coverage determination with policy citations, a damage report with cost tiers, a fraud risk score, and a clear recommended action — all in one view.

Capabilities

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Purpose-built for independent adjusters and regional agencies who can't afford slow, manual processing.

📄
Policy-to-Claim Matcher

Upload any homeowner, auto, or commercial policy PDF alongside a claim summary. iLow reads both and returns a structured list of covered and not-covered items — each with a direct citation to the policy language.

Supports any standard PDF policy format
Direct citations to policy sections
Overall coverage determination with confidence score
📷
Damage Image Assessor

Upload roof, interior, or vehicle damage photos. iLow identifies the damage type, assigns a severity rating (Minor to Total Loss), classifies affected materials by replacement cost tier, and flags anomalies worth investigating.

Damage type: hail, water, fire, collision, wind
Material cost tiers: Low / Medium / High
Estimated repair scope and red flags
🔍
Fraud Flag Screener

Run claim details through iLow's AI fraud analysis. It checks for new-policy claims, high-frequency claimants, date inconsistencies, high-risk zip codes, inflated amounts, and vague or suspicious descriptions.

Risk score from 0–100 with explanation
Individual red flags with severity ratings
