How It Works

Three steps. One decision.

iLow walks adjusters through the full review pipeline — from intake to recommended action — in under a minute.

1
Upload the Claim

Drop in a policy PDF, paste a claim summary, upload damage photos, or fill in claim details. iLow accepts everything adjusters already work with — no system change required.

2
AI Cross-Checks Everything

iLow AI cross-checks policy language, damage images, and claim data simultaneously — reading coverage clauses, classifying damage severity, and flagging fraud signals in a single pass.

3
Get a Structured Decision

Receive a coverage determination with direct policy citations, a damage report with cost tiers, a fraud risk score, and a recommended action — all in one structured, auditable output.
